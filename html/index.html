About Us

"Our story is one of a shared obsession: to honor the hard work of coffee farmers and bring their incredible, ethically-sourced beans to your cup. It started in a small garage with a single roaster and a big dream. We travel the world, building direct relationships with farmers who share our commitment to sustainable practices and incredible flavor. Our mission is to roast each batch with care, unlocking the unique story of its origin. Every bag is a testament to that journey, and a promise of freshness delivered from our roastery to your home."
